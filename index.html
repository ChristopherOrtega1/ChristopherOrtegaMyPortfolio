Portfolio

To demonstrate some of my experience in the field, I would like to briefly explain some of the projects that I have taken part of throughout my years as a student

  • Real-estate Prediction Tool

    Geographically weighted regression was used to be able to create a model that is able to accurately predict the prices of houses in the metropolitan area in Guadalajara, Jalisco. The app itself is a webpage where users can add their own listings to the database of homes, or make queries to the model for predictions.

  • Hardware Shop Leo Database App

    Application made for a friend's uncle, who happens to be the owner of a small hardware shop. The app itself is an interface to access and modify the contents of a MySQL database of all the items in the shop, as well as the information and activity of the individual employees.

  • Cancer Cell Classifier

    Using a dataset of known readings of different cells taken from a multitude of people, a data model is trained with a multilayer perceptron neural network. The model allows for future test cells to be classified as either being indicative of the presence of cancer, or the contrary.
